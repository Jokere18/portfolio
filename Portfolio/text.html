About Me

I chose mobile development because I dream of launching a startup with an app that revolutionizes how people manage their health. My vision is to create an application that can take a photo of any meal and instantly analyze whether it's diabetic-friendly, providing users with crucial health insights at their fingertips.

Beyond meal analysis, I envision the app suggesting personalized recipes and recommending foods to buy based on individual dietary needs. This isn't just about technology—it's about empowering people to make informed decisions about their health and well-being.

I'm just beginning my mobile development journey, but I bring a rich background in web development and AI engineering. This unique combination allows me to approach mobile app development with a deep understanding of both user experience and cutting-edge AI capabilities.

My passion lies in solving real-world problems through code. Every line I write is driven by the belief that technology should make life better, healthier, and more accessible for everyone.
