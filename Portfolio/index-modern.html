About Me

I'm passionate about creating technology that makes a real difference in people's lives. While I have a particular love for mobile development, I enjoy working across the full stack—from crafting beautiful user interfaces to building robust backend systems that power intelligent applications.

My dream project is launching a startup with an app that revolutionizes health management: one that can take a photo of any meal and instantly analyze whether it's diabetic-friendly, then suggest personalized recipes and shopping recommendations based on individual dietary needs.

Beyond app development, I love diving into data science and machine learning projects. I recently built a Music Popularity Predictor that analyzes Kendrick Lamar's tracks to understand what makes his songs successful—combining my interests in AI, data analysis, and music.

Whether it's mobile apps, web applications, or backend data processing, I'm driven by the same core belief: technology should solve real problems and improve lives.
